Bio

I am a 2nd year PhD student in Statistics at UC Davis, working with Miles Lopes. I am broadly interested in high-dimensional statistics and theoretical machine learning.

I received my BA and MA in Statistics at UC Santa Barbara, where I was supervised by Alexander Petersen, Guo Yu, Sang-Yun Oh, and Wendy Meiring on particular projects. Before coming to UC Davis, I worked at the Artificial Intelligence in Biomedical Imaging Lab at the University of Pennsylvania.
